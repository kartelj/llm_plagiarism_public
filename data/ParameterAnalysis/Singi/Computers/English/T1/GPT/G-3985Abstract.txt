Education is a crucial element for the economic and social advancement of any community in the 21st century. Traditional educational methodologies are increasingly becoming obsolete as they struggle to address the contemporary needs of students. With modern technology, mobile learning has emerged as a progressive approach to education, providing the convenience of anytime, anywhere accessibility. Education sector too has been swept by this trend. Technological advancements like computers, internet services, mobile devices, and specifically cloud computing, have offered a wealth of fresh possibilities to the academic institutions.
