In the 21st century, education is a crucial component of a community's economic and social growth. Traditional educational methods are largely irrelevant, as they fail to cater to students' requirements. Mobile learning serves as a fresh avenue for education, permitting access from anywhere and anytime. The sphere of education has not been exempt from this trend. The advent of computers, internet facilities, mobile gadgets, and especially cloud computing, has endowed educational establishments with numerous novel capabilities.
