The thesis explores the theoretical and practical examination of large public corporations in the Republic of Serbia, focusing on their operational efficiency, a critical assessment of privatizations in the Republic of Serbia and transitioning countries, proposed privatization models, and strategic privatization recommendations. The study establishes the primary role of public corporations, compares efficiency between private and state-owned public corporations, scrutinizes corporate governance in public corporations, and offers a global perspective on the significance and role of public corporations and their privatization. It presents an examination of modern management tools' application and their influence on efficiency improvements. The paper emphasizes the relevance of public-private partnerships and the need for corporatization of public corporations. The significance of privatization in enhancing efficiency and performance of public corporations is illustrated. The stance on privatizations and corporatization of the public enterprise "Elektroprivreda" and the joint-stock company "Telekom" are also outlined.
