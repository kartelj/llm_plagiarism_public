In today's dynamic tourism market, the fluctuating demands of discerning tourists are clearly visible. Few global tourist destinations possess unique attractions that draw tourists and steer travel demand at local, regional, national, and international levels. This is a key reason why tourist destinations are increasingly vying for superiority, establishing a competitive edge through superior offerings compared to similar regional destinations. Hence, it's crucial to evaluate and scrutinize the state of tourism in Vojvodina as a tourist destination, as well as the perspectives of employees in both public and private tourism companies, to derive insights that can enhance Vojvodina's competitive standing against comparable destinations nearby. Utilizing the value chain as a strategy for competitive advantage has yielded certain findings that suggest potential pathways for the future growth of tourism in Vojvodina.
