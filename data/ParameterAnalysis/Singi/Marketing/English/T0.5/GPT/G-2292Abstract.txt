The dissertation explores the theoretical and practical examination of large public businesses in Serbia, focusing specifically on their operational effectiveness, a critical assessment of privatizations in Serbia and transitional countries, proposed privatization models, and strategic privatization recommendations. The study defines the primary role of public enterprises, compares the efficiency of private and state ownership in public enterprises, scrutinizes corporate governance in public enterprises, and offers a global perspective on the significance and role of public enterprises and their privatization. It also reviews the use of modern management tools and their influence on increasing efficiency. The study pays special attention to the relevance of public-private partnerships and the need for corporatization in public enterprises. It illustrates the importance of privatization in enhancing efficiency and improving the performance of public enterprises. The study also presents viewpoints on the privatization and corporatization of the public enterprise "Elektroprivreda" and the joint-stock company "Telekom".
