The contemporary tourism market is characterized by constant shifts in consumer demand, with tourists being particularly discerning customers. Almost every tourist destination globally has unique attractions that draw tourists and influence travel demand on local, regional, national, and international scales. This has led to increased competition among tourist destinations, each vying for a competitive edge through high-quality offerings relative to similar destinations. It is therefore crucial to evaluate and scrutinize the status of Vojvodina's tourism sector, as well as the perspectives of employees in both the public and private tourism sectors. This will enable the extraction of insights that could bolster Vojvodina's competitive standing relative to comparable destinations. By leveraging the value chain strategy for competitive advantage, we have identified potential future growth avenues for tourism in Vojvodina.
