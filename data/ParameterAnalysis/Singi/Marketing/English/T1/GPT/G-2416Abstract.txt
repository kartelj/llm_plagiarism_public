In today's dynamic tourism marketplace, the fluctuating daily preferences of discerning tourists are quite noticeable. There are few tourist hotspots globally that possess unique attractions that draw in travelers and influence demand at the local, regional, national, and even international level. Thus, competition among vacation destinations is inevitable, with each striving to outshine the others by providing superior services and experiences. Therefore, it is crucial to evaluate and study the tourism scenario in Vojvodina and the perspectives of personnel in both governmental and private tourism enterprises. This will equips us with insights helpful in ensuring that Vojvodina gains an edge over comparable destinations nearby. Through the use of the value chain as a strategic tool for gaining a competitive edge, we have discerned specific outcomes that denote the potential paths for Vojvodina's tourism sector to develop further.
