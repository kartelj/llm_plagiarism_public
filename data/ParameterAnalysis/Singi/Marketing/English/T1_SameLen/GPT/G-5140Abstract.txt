The rivalry within the tourism market is escalating. Tourist destinations with superior offerings, and hence higher value, are progressively becoming the favored choices among tourists. Any actions taken to cater to tourists at spas make up the value chain of spa travel. The parties conducting these activities are the links in this chain, delivering a comprehensive spa experience via a series of actions and activities. Tourism-oriented businesses strive for enhanced activities to offer a superior partial product. The collective efforts of all businesses at the spa destination provide tourists with a complete spa product composed of a range of partial products. A comparison and assessment of spa locations in renowned European countries with those in Serbia shed light on the significance and role of Serbian spas. This comparative study has exposed the principal strengths and weaknesses in Serbia's spa value chain. The competitive edge of Serbian spas lies in their extraordinary natural assets, which if leveraged systematically could significantly boost tourism.
