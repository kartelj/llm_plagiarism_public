The competition within the tourism industry is becoming more fierce. Tourist spots that offer superior services and value are becoming the preferred choices for travelers. All efforts made to provide services to tourists at spa destinations form the value chain of that destination. Those who carry out these activities are the links in the value chain, delivering a complete spa experience through a series of actions and activities. Businesses involved in providing tourist services strive to improve their activities to deliver a higher quality product. The collective efforts of all businesses at the spa destination provide tourists with a comprehensive spa product composed of various partial products. An analysis of spa destinations in renowned European countries, compared with those in Serbia, highlights the position, role, and significance of Serbian spas. This comparative analysis uncovers the main strengths and weaknesses in the value chain of Serbian spas. The competitive edge of Serbian spas lies in their extraordinary natural potential, which could be better leveraged for tourism through a systematic approach.
