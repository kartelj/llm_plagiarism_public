The contemporary tourism market is characterized by constant fluctuations in demand, driven by increasingly discerning tourists. Few global tourist destinations possess unique attractions that draw tourists and influence travel demand at local, regional, national, and international levels. This has led to heightened competition among tourist destinations, each striving to gain a competitive edge through superior offerings. Therefore, it's crucial to evaluate and scrutinize the state of tourism in Vojvodina, as well as the perspectives of employees in both public and private tourism enterprises. This will help in formulating strategies to enhance Vojvodina's competitiveness against similar destinations. Utilizing the value chain as a strategy for competitive advantage has yielded results that suggest potential avenues for the future growth of tourism in Vojvodina.
