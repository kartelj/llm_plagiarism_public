Modern businesses are characterized by many profound technological changes. Enhancing business communication through reference innovations such as contact centers is crucial. Contact centers play a pivotal role in ensuring productivity and effectiveness as they are judged by clients and potential clients based on their services, the bank's operations and products. To enhance the efficiency of contact centers, certain aspects need to be identified: type of contact center, technology, communication channels, employee training, workforce size, shift operations, and employee supervision. All these factors greatly impact business communication. The formation of the first contact centers started in the early fifties of the previous century, leading to numerous transformations that have consequently influenced business communication. Business communication can thus drive the evolution of contact centers across various organizations in numerous ways.
