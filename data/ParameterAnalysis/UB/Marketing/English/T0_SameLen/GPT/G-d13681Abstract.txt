Modern business is characterized by numerous significant technological shifts. Enhancing business communication through referential innovation, such as contact centers, is crucial. The contact center plays a vital role in ensuring productivity and effectiveness as clients and potential clients assess the contact center, the bank's services, and products. To enhance the contact center's performance, certain factors need to be identified, such as the contact center type, technology, communication channels, employee training, employee count, shift work, and employee supervision. All these factors significantly impact business communication. The formation of the first contact centers began in the early fifties of the previous century, leading to many changes that have influenced business communication. Business communication can, in many ways, advance contact centers with varying organization.
