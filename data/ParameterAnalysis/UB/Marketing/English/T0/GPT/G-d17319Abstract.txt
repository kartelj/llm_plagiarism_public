The primary objective of the Doctoral Dissertation research is to establish a comprehensive research project framework aimed at enhancing the decision-making and data mining model for various analyses, including association rule mining and prediction. The research also aims to utilize the results to make effective management decisions. The study focuses on analyzing and applying a system of association rules to enhance the business decision-making model for senior managers, with the ultimate goal of making the most effective decisions. The research employs modern scientific methods from the field of business intelligence. The central hypothesis, "Improvement of the business decision-making model is possible through a system of association rules," was confirmed during the study. The research underscores the significance of business intelligence in developing a model that can boost the effectiveness of managerial decision-making. The use of association rules for research purposes has enormous potential in the business world. A business decision-making model based on association rules was developed and presented, demonstrating the popularity and potential of this business intelligence field. The research concludes with remarks and recommendations for future studies, aiming to make a substantial scientific and professional contribution towards enhancing business decision-making.
