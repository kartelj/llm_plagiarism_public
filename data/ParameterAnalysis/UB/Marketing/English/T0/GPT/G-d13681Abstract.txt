Modern business is characterized by numerous significant technological advancements. Enhancing business communication is crucial and can be achieved through referential innovation such as contact centers. The role of a contact center is pivotal in ensuring productivity and effectiveness as it is evaluated by clients and potential clients based on the services and products of the bank. To enhance the performance of the contact center, certain factors need to be identified. These include the type of contact center, technology, communication channels, employee training, the number of employees, shift work, and employee supervision. All these factors significantly impact business communication. The formation of the first contact centers began in the early fifties of the last century, leading to numerous transformations in business communication. In many ways, business communication can be enhanced by contact centers with varying organizational structures.
