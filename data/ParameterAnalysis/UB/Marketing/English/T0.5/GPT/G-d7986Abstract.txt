This paper explores marketing strategies that aim to foster customer trust in the digital realm. To comprehend the factors that impact these strategies, we have examined existing theories and literature on online commerce and customer trust, with particular emphasis on elements that influence trust in a digital setting. Through investigating trust elements, we have identified factors that significantly affect online customer trust. 

Using these factors and existing theoretical models, we have developed a framework for cultivating customer trust in a digital environment. This framework not only offers theoretical guidelines in the form of marketing strategies but also provides comprehensive recommendations for their practical application. 

The contribution of this dissertation is multifaceted: (1) it organizes literature on general trust, trust-building models, and online trust; (2) it provides a thorough review of global and national practices, offering precise insights into the field of online commerce; (3) it establishes a conceptual trust framework, breaking down trust into conceptual units with detailed descriptions. This framework offers crucial insights into online trust and serves as a foundation for further strategy, activity, and tool development for building online trust. It can also be used as a model for analyzing similar concepts like loyalty and satisfaction; (4) it identifies and systematically presents factors that influence customer trust in a digital setting. This provides fundamental guidelines for businesses aiming to boost online shopper confidence; (5) it presents a theoretical framework for crafting strategies in a digital environment. This comprehensive guide has broader application potential beyond the dissertation's subject matter. It can be applied to strategy formulation related to other aspects of online commerce, as well as strategies concerning online education, online banking, and many other concepts; (6) it creates a trust-building framework that includes strategies and specific implementation activities. Applying this framework in practice can enhance online consumer trust, reduce perceived risk, and increase perceived usefulness and ease of use of the online store.
