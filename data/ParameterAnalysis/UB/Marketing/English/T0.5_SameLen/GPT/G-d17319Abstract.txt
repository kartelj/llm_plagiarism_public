The primary objective of the Doctoral Dissertation research is to establish a comprehensive research project framework for enhancing decision-making and data mining models for various analyses: association rule mining and prediction, and the utilization of these results for effective management decisions. The research aims to analyze and apply an association rule system to refine the business decision-making model for top-tier business system managers, to achieve the most effective decisions. The research employed contemporary scientific methods from the business intelligence field. The main hypothesis: "The business decision-making model can be enhanced by an association rule system" was validated during the research. The significance of business intelligence in developing a model that can boost the efficacy of managerial decision-making is emphasized. The use of association rules for research purposes holds great potential in business. A business decision-making model using association rules was developed and presented. It's demonstrated that this business intelligence area is highly sought-after and holds substantial potential. Concluding thoughts and future research recommendations have been provided to make a meaningful scientific and professional contribution towards enhancing business decision-making.
